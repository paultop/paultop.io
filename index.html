My Story

I work best at the intersection of technical products, enterprise customers, and new markets.

I started my career in mechanical engineering and manufacturing, where I learned to think in systems, constraints, and root causes. That foundation eventually pulled me into enterprise technology sales, where I found I could help technical teams and business leaders understand each other.

I joined Confluent early in 2017 and moved to Sydney to help build the APAC market. Over the next seven years, I worked across Australia’s enterprise landscape helping customers adopt complex platforms in data, testing, and software delivery.

The common thread throughout my career has been understanding deeply technical products, simplifying complexity, and helping organizations turn engineering capability into real-world adoption.

I’m currently interested in AI infrastructure, data systems, and technical products with strong engineering cultures.

Some of that journey is mapped here: Where I've Been.
