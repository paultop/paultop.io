My Story

I work best at the intersection of technical products, enterprise customers, and new markets.

The common thread throughout my career has been understanding deeply technical products, simplifying complexity, and helping organizations turn engineering capability into real-world adoption.

I’m currently interested in AI infrastructure, data systems, and technical products with strong engineering cultures.
